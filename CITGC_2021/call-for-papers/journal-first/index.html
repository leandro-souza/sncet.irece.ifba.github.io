Submissão de Trabalhos

O I CITGC conta com o Sistema de Gerenciamento de Eventos (SGE) plataforma de suporte para submissão de trabalhos SGE. Por meio do SGE, os autores de artigos científicos aceitos em periódicos parceiros serão convidados a apresentar seus trabalhos no CITGC, proporcionando assim uma oportunidade para os autores se envolverem diretamente com a comunidade e oferecendo aos participantes do CITGC uma dimensão adicional para o caminho de pesquisa program.

The journals that support the journal-first model as partners with ICSA is:

SGE

Uma submissão para a convocação ICSA 2020 para apresentações de artigos diários deve cumprir os seguintes critérios:

  • O artigo está no escopo da conferência.
  • O artigo relata resultados de pesquisa completamente novos e / ou apresenta novas contribuições que se estendem significativamente e não foram relatadas anteriormente em trabalhos anteriores.
  • O artigo não estende o trabalho anterior apenas com provas ou algoritmos adicionais (ou outros detalhes apresentados para integridade), resultados empíricos adicionais ou pequenos aprimoramentos ou variantes dos resultados apresentados no trabalho anterior.
  • O artigo não foi apresentado e não está sendo considerado para programas de publicação de outras conferências.

How to submit

The authors of a paper that respects these criteria are invited to submit a one-page presentation proposal consisting of the paper’s title, the paper’s authors, an extended abstract, and a pointer to the original journal paper at the journal’s Web site. Use the title “Extended Abstract – title of you original paper”. All submissions must conform to the Author Instructions (please ignore Info about double-blind review process). If the journal paper extends previously published work (such as, a tool demo or a poster), then the proposal must include a section listing the main contribution that are novel to the journal paper. If the paper is not online yet, then specify so.

Please submit your extended abstract to the EasyChair submission system.

Evaluation and selection

Authors will be invited to present their paper at ICSA 2020 after a check that the paper satisfies the above listed criteria. As the papers have already been reviewed and accepted by the journals, they will not be reviewed again for technical content. In case an exceptionally high number of submissions is received, not all papers will be selected. Priority will be given to the papers that:

  • Increase opportunities for authors to attend ICSA, who might not otherwise attend. In other words, priority will be given to papers with authors that have no (or fewer) papers accepted at the other ICSA tracks.
  • Best fit the technical program, offering a balance across the conference topics.

If there is further need to select from papers with the same priority, then they will be selected based on a lottery.

Important Dates

  • Submission deadline: January 30, 2020 AoE (Anywhere on Earth, UTC-12)
  • Notification: February 07, 2020

Presentation

If a submission is accepted for the journal-first program, at least one author of the associated journal paper must register and attend the conference to present the paper. A journal-first presentation will be scheduled in a session with topically-related Technical Track or other appropriate track. The journal-first manuscripts are published through the journals and will not be part of the ICSA proceedings. The journal-first papers will be listed in the conference program.
