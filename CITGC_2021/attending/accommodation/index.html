Accommodation

The conference will be held at the Mercure Salvador Rio Vermelho Hotel

Address:
R. da Fonte do Boi, 215, Rio Vermelho,
Salvador- BA, Brazil, 41940-360

Room type and price:
  • Standard Single Room: R$ 261 + TAX
  • Standard Double Room: R$ 305 + TAX
  • Superior Single Room (seafront): R$ 301 + TAX
  • Superior Double Room (seafront): R$ 345 + TAX

The hotel is situated in the seaside district of Rio Vermelho, in one of Salvador's best regions. Close to Barra Beach and to the most beautiful seafront beaches. 12.4 miles (20 km) from Deputado Luis Eduardo Magalhaes Airport and 9.3 miles (15 km) from the city center.
