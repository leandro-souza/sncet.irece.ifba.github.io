Registration

Registration is now open!


Registration Deadlines
Author registration: 10 February 2020
Early registration: 16 February 2020
Cancellation/Refund: 25 February 2020

Cancellation and refund policy
In the case of cancellation before 25 February 2020, we refund 40% of the conference price. Transferring the ticket to another person is free of charge.

Types of Registration
Authors have to register using a non-student ticket by the author registration deadline 10 February 2020 (for conference papers) and by the Early registration deadline 16 February 2020 (for workshop papers). Otherwise papers will not be published in proceedings and IEEE digital library.
